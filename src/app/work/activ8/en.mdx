## Overview
<br/>

With increasing social isolation and difficulty forming genuine connections, Activ8 emerged to facilitate meetups between people with common interests in a natural and safe way.
<br/>

Our solution needed to solve two main challenges: creating an efficient matching system based on multiple criteria and ensuring user safety and reliability through verification and ratings.
<br/>

We developed an algorithm that analyzes profiles, interests, location, and activity history to suggest the best connections. We also implemented a robust verification and moderation system to keep the community safe.
